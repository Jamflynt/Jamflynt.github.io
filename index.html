About Me

Hello World!

Great to meet you! My name is Jamie Flynt and I am very glad you would like to know more about me. I grew up just outside of Greensboro, North Carolina. After attending some college at North Carolina State University and floating around from job to job to make ends meet for the better part of my adult life, in fall of 2020 I decided to make a change and moved to Covington, Kentucky after being offered room and board in exchange for helping to restore and maintain two properties owned by a friend. I could not be happier to call Northern Kentucky my new home.


After my move, I took some time off to really reflect on what I wanted to do professionally. It was when I started making a WordPress website for my small Cottage Food Bakery that I stumbled upon a passion for Web Design and Development. I simply couldn’t get enough of it. After completing my website I went on a search for ways to make this my career. I applied and was selected for enrollment in Commonwealth Coders, a 16 week intensive web development training course in partnership with BC Skills Academy and the Workforce Board of Northern Kentucky.


I am now highly proficient in HTML5, CSS3, JavaScript and many more skills (you can see a full list or the major web development skills below in the Skills section). My background professional background is diverse, ranging from construction and production jobs to event management. However, these all require communication, organization, problem solving, the ability to think and learn quickly, and so many more skills that are transferable to the world of Web Development. I love that there is always something new to learn and a new hurtle to get over and I cannot wait to start my career in this field.

Check out my resume here

When I am not behind the computer screen coding I am still out there trying to learn new things to push and better myself. You can find me baking cookies for my small side business, Stove Pipe Confections Company. I am currently working on getting a delivery option up and running for my more loyal customers. I also make, design, and sell furniture pieces, mostly with found or reclaimed wood from the surrounding areas of Covington. This is a new passion I discovered over the last couple of years. I am an avid hiker, photographer, biker, runner, and simply love being outdoors. In short if its a challenge and I can learn something new, I am interested. I really appreciate you taking the time to read my page and taking an interest in who I am, what I do, and what I aspire to be. I look forward to working with you.
